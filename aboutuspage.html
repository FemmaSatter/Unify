About us  

In a world of isolation, we believe reaching out to others is vital because only then we can build a supportive community.

The story of Unify started one year ago. We noticed that all parfum brands have the same goal: making people feel unique. Looking at recent events we feel that ‘’being unique’’ might not be something people need anymore. Instead seeking out a connection to others is more important. That’s where we decided to create a parfum specially made for groups of people.We believe Unify can give you the feeling of connection when wearing the same scent.
